My steps to creative career

I strongly believe that talent is overrated.
What matters is the desire to create.

Since childhood, I have been drawn to drawing, and later, as a teenager, I loved spending my time playing with Photoshop.

Studying geography and social studies sparked my appreciation for the world and its people.

With a master’s degree in education, I now seek real-life experiences beyond theory.

Working in England, I fell in love with the rich variety of cultures. Later, moving to Montana, USA, I was captivated by the local life and natural beauty, just as I was while working as a photographer in Banff National Park, Canada.

When not traveling, I gained valuable experience with international companies.

I worked in HR and with geographical systems, but one of my favorite jobs was in a vineyard. My passion for graphic design grew while working for a kids' photography company in Brno.

Over the past 5 years, I've focused on graphic design, working as an in-house designer for RWS. In 2024, I completed an 8-month UX/UI design course.

My journey hasn't been a straight path but an engaging one, with each turn bringing me closer to a creative future and my true self.
